Bird Postcard Series

ILLUSTRATION, 2014

Like flowers, each bird symbolizes different meanings, and, using those symbolization, I drew illustrations for a series of postcards. In the postcards, I drew bluebird, cardinal, canary, chicken, and robin. They represent, in order, harbinger of happiness, love, joy, fear, and rebirth.
