Five Diagrams

POSTERS, 2015

After selecting a place right next to the RISD architecture building, I carefully examined the spot and drew five diagrams using my observational sketches and notes. Each diagram shows things people carry during 5 minutes, colors of the cars passed by within the 5 minutes, different shapes and sizes of signs, number of people who sat on the benches within the 2 hours, and the motion path of ducks and boats on the river.
