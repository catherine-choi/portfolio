I'mperfect Campaign

BRANDING, 2016

I rebranded a campaign about body confidence, called "Be Real,” and renamed it to be called “I'mperfect," which conveys a message that imperfection is perfection. Through my campaign, I wanted to tell people that we all are equally important no matter how we look. Everybody is different; therefore, every body is unique! Throughout the identity system, I treated the apostrophe in the logotype as one individual person, and personified it. Any typeface could be used for the apostrophe according to different usage.

DRAWING CLASS
I'mperfect campaign will have drawing classes where anyone can join and draw each other. People can also choose to draw themselves or anyone in the event.

MOTION GRAPHIC
The campaign also has a counseling program that people can tell their concerns about their appearances.
