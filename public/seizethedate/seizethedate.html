Seize The Date!

UI/UX, 2016

Seize the date is an app for keeping track of expiration date. Users can record expiration dates of various subjects, including food, cosmetic, medicine, ticket, coupon, and even their crucial appointment. The app has a customizable feature that users can easily design their own categories, and it keeps alerting upcoming expiration dates. While focusing on the humorous aspect of this app, I also tried to help users not to waste their items by introducing proper ways of preservation and a myriad of different usages, and encouraging donation of unneeded items.

1. SIGNING IN
Users will see the grid of different categories when they sign up, and they can customize it. The hamburger menu leads to the list of menu with a profile picture.

2. CUSTOMIZING GRID
When users click the plus icon, they can customize their own grid by dragging icons from the bottom or clicking x icon on the left corner of each category.

3. ADDING ITMES
Each category has a list of items, and users can either manually take photos or scan barcodes to add items.

4. SAVING ITEMS
On the detail page of an item, the app introduces how to preserve, use, and share items with other people. For instance, if the page is about an apple, the apple page has three buttons at the bottom, and each button shows how to preserve apples, how to cook, and where people can donate them.

5. BEING NOTIFIED
The app has a notification feature that keeps informing users about expiration dates of items.
