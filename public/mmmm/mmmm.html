Marvelous Museum
of Mesmerizing Miniatures

BRANDING, 2016

Rebranding the Museum of Miniatures, located in Arizona, I wanted to design a whole new identity system that is playful and catchy. Since it’s a miniature museum, my focus was to make the museum fun and friendly. I called my museum Marvelous Museum of Mesmerizing Miniatures. Considering the idea of miniatures, things represented on a smaller scale, I intentionally made the name of my museum long, and the name could be abbreviated as MMMM. For the mark, I used the negative space of the letter M and made a dynamic logo, in which the shape of logo keeps changing for different uses.

MMMM X TED: “Small Thoughts to Big Ideas”
Partnering with TED Talks, MMMM will have a series of talks about how small thoughts can become big ideas. Popular figures, such as a founder of Airbnb, who started from a small idea that grew into a big one, will be invited to give a talk.

MMMM SPECIAL COUNTRY EXHIBITION
MMMM will also have a special miniature exhibition of different countries and their culture. Each exhibition will hold collections varying from food to architecture. Using distinct color palettes from the country flags, I designed a series of ticket as an example.
