Canal Walk

ANIMATION, 2016

The Canal Walk, one of the most memorable places during my college life, is a sidewalk along the Providence River and my major studio. Based on my observation, I drew eight maps and base on those maps, I created an animation that shows my feeling and experience at that place since my freshman year at RISD. I tried to convey what I saw, what I heard, what I smelled, what I felt, and what I did.
